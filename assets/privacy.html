1. Information We Collect

When you use Diagnosis AI, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: name, email address, password when you register.
  • Authentication data: credentials via Google Sign-In or email/password.
  • Health-related inputs: body area selections and responses you provide for AI analysis.
  • Usage data: features used, time spent in the app.
🔒 We never collect your data without your explicit consent. Health inputs are used for analysis only and are not shared with advertisers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account.
  • To provide AI-powered symptom analysis.
  • To verify your identity and keep your account secure.
  • To send email verification messages.
  • To comply with legal GDPR obligations.

We do not use your health data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We only share your data in these situations:

  • Service providers: Firebase (Google) for authentication and database storage.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or court order.
  • Safety: To protect the safety of our users.

We never sell your personal data to anyone.

4. Data Security

We take security seriously and use these measures to protect your data:

  • Firebase Authentication with OAuth 2.0 for secure sign-in.
  • API key is not public and is secured properly.
  • Your passwords are not visible in Firebase studio.
  • We review our security practices regularly.
⚠️ No system is 100% secure. If you think your account is compromised, contact us right away at SKostanian@uclan.ac.uk

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights over your personal data:

  • Access: Get a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Fix wrong or outdated information.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Export your data in a readable format.
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time.

To use any of these rights, email us at SKostanian@uclan.ac.uk.

6. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as needed:

  • Account data is kept while your account is active.
  • After account deletion, your data is removed within 5 days.
  • Anonymized analytics data may be kept longer.
  • Backups are deleted within 90 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Diagnosis AI is not for users under 13 (in US/UK), under 16 (in European Union) and under 14 in Russia. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

If we discover a child has registered, we will delete their data immediately. Please contact us if you believe this has happened.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make important changes, we will:

  • Show a notice inside the app.
  • Send an email to registered users.
  • Update the "Last updated" date above.

Continuing to use the app after changes means you accept the new policy.

Have questions about your privacy?

Send us an email and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
